About this Graph

A packed circle graph is similar to a tree map in that it shows hierarchical data. Compared to other hierarchical graphs, the packed circle chart uses circles to represent the item, or node. Child nodes are linked to their parent nodes by being placed inside of them. One thing to consider with this graph is how to label items and the importance of labels. This kind of graph is helpful if the focus is the children. It is more difficult to get informaation on the parent nodes or intermediate nodes with this graph.

Marks and Channels

Marks

Marks include area which also acts as a containment node.

Channels

Channels include the size of the area as well as color. The spatial placement is also shown in terms of which circles are inside others.
