About this Graph

A packed circle graph is similar to a tree map in that it shows hierarchical data. Compared to other hierarchical graphs, the packed circle chart uses circles to represent the item, or node. Child nodes are linked to their parent nodes by being placed inside of them. One thing to consider with this graph is how to label items and the importance of labels.

Marks and Channels

Marks

Marks include containment as well as area.

Channels

Channels include the size of the area as well as color.
