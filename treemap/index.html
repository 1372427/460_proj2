About this Graph

A tree map shows hierarchical information. Similar to a packed circle graph, the treemap shows child elements as being inside their parent elements. However, a treemap uses rectangles rather than circles and therefore might be more organized in terms of being able to read the groups quickly. Using padding for the treemap also allows for better labelling. While the packed circle is useful for focusing on leafs and as such may only have leafs labelled, the treemap likely has all levels labelled as in the above example.

Marks and Channels

Marks

Marks include area.

Channels

Channels include area, as well as spatial placement, and potentially color if used to categorize elements.
